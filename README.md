# Credit Card Customer Dashboard

## Overview

This project presents an interactive customer analytics dashboard built in Looker Studio to explore credit card customer behavior, demographics, spending patterns, and risk indicators.

The dashboard helps stakeholders understand customer characteristics, monitor business performance, and identify opportunities for customer retention and revenue growth.

---

## Business Objective

Financial institutions manage thousands of customers with different spending habits, income levels, and risk profiles. Understanding these differences is essential for improving customer experience, increasing profitability, and reducing potential risks.

This dashboard was developed to provide a comprehensive view of customer behavior and support data-driven decision-making.

---

## Dashboard Features

### Customer Overview

Monitor key business metrics including:

- Number of Customers
- Total Transaction Volume
- Total Transaction Amount
- Average Satisfaction Score

### Customer Demographics

Analyze customer distribution based on:

- Occupation
- Education Level
- Marital Status
- State

### Customer Revenue Profile

Evaluate customer contributions through:

- Transaction Amount
- Annual Fees
- Interest Earned
- Transaction Volume

### Customer Risk Profile

Monitor customer risk indicators and delinquency patterns across demographic groups.

### Spending Analysis

Understand customer spending behavior across different expense categories, including:

- Food
- Grocery
- Travel
- Entertainment
- Fuel
- Bills

### Interactive Filtering

Allow users to explore customer data dynamically through dashboard filters.
